Trinetra is a free Chrome extension that automatically scans Terms & Conditions, flags dangerous clauses in red, shows safe ones in green, and cites the exact Indian law that protects you. The AI runs on our server — nothing extra needed on your device.

🧩 Add Trinetra to Chrome FREE
The AI runs on our server — nothing to install on your PC except the extension itself. Just download, unzip, and load into Chrome. Takes 60 seconds.
  1. 1
    Click the Download button below — the ZIP saves to your Downloads folder Contains 5 files: manifest.json, popup.html, popup.js, content.js, background.js
  2. 2
    Right-click the ZIP → Extract All → choose a folder On Windows: right-click trinetra.zip → Extract All → Extract
  3. 3
    Open Chrome and go to: chrome://extensions
  4. 4
    Toggle Developer Mode ON — top right corner
  5. 5
    Click Load Unpacked → select the extracted trinetra_ext folder The 👁️ icon appears in Chrome toolbar — open any T&C page and it works automatically ✓
